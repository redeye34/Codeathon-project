Our Story

Every idea begins with a problem — and for us, it was watching people around us lose their time in never-ending queues, missed reservations, and frustrating last-minute cancellations. From clinics to restaurants, from hospitals to salons, we saw the struggle everywhere.

In January 2023, Faazil sat down and asked a simple question: "Why is booking a service still so complicated?"

"I built Atras not as a company, but as a solution. A solution that empowers users to book services with ease, and helps businesses manage queues smarter."

That question sparked something deeper. What began as a casual discussion quickly turned into a mission — to create a platform that simplifies service booking, removes the waiting, and gives time back to people.
